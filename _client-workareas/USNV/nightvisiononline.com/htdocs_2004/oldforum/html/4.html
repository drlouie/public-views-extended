Thermal problems
by

Through my many interactions with the actual equipment designers and users in the field, they have pointed other disadvantages of thermal vs image intensified equipment:
- Bad performance in certain weather conditions: fog, falling snow, morning dew etc, when those atmosphercic conditions tend to equalise temperatures.
- Inability to detect idle equipment (i.e tank, artillery etc.)
- Vey small field of view (it's very hard to get even close to 40 or 60 degrees available in modern NV goggles)
