The NiteMax Ultra Viewer is a portable digital night vision viewing device. The digital technology which allows the NiteMax Ultra to work in low light is currently used in high end surveillance systems and has now been adapted for use in a hand-held, battery powered unit. Unlike image intensified night vision technology, the NiteMax Ultra Viewer can be used in any lighting condition without damage to the system. The NiteMax Ultra utilizes specially manufactured infrared diodes that are focused and tuned to the sensor to allow for maximum distance and clarity in low light applications.

The NiteMax Ultra is a versatile device that can perform in many ways better than current image intensified night vision viewers. By allowing the user to easily attach the NiteMax Ultra to video capture devices such as camcorders, laptops or VCR units The NiteMax Ultra can help record night images clearly and efficiently. The NiteMax Ultra can also be powered by auxiliary AC/DC power sources. The image is displayed on a 2.5" TFT-LCD, the same display that is used in millions of camcorder products worldwide. This high-resolution display allows for single and multiple user viewing without the typical eyestrain of current night vision devices. The NiteMax Ultra is perfect for surveillance, nature studies, or will turn you old camcorder into a state-of-the-art night vision camera (RCA input jack required on camcorder).
