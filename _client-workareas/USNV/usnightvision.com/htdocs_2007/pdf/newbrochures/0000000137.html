Tactical vehicle operations at night can often be difficult due to a lack of illumination. Driving with just NVGs not only slows down your element's rate of movement, but also makes recognition more difficult and increases your risk of fratricide. U.S. Night Vision's Blackout Filter KitsTM for the HMMWV, Army 5 ton, Army 2 ton and the Marine Corps 7 ton are an instance force multiplier for nighttime tactical vehicle operations. Each Blackout Infrared Filter provide as much IR illumination, when seen through night vision, as a vehicle's headlight seen by the naked eye at night; facilitating a safer, faster and more efficient means of travel. The newly redesigned Blackout Filter kits come complete with two "Quick-Detach" Infrared headlight filters, two Infrared taillight filters and blackout covers for all running lights. Parts for each individual kit are listed below. The USNV BF-10 Blackout IR Filter Kit comes with: 2 Ea. IR headlight filters (850 nm) 2 Ea. IR tail light filters (850 nm) 2 Ea. orange headlight covers 4 Ea. running light covers. Nylon kit bag, instruction manual and mounting screws included.
