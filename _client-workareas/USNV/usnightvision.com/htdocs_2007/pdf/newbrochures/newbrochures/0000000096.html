The AQUILA 2I is a low profile night vision weapon sight which has 4X objectives with ballistic controls suitable for matching with individual, crew served, and sniper weapons. Because the AQUILA 2I is a straight-through sight design it offers excellent cheek-weld characteristics on the lower carbine profile with a flat mounting rail in the 5.56 mm caliber family. The 6X version features specific ballistic controls that assists in the proper acquisition of targets with longer range 7.62 mm crew served weapons. Windage adjustments include slip scale with 0.5 MOA tactile clicks. Elevation adjustment includes ballistic drop compensating scale and 1.0 MOA tactile clicks. AQUILA 2I can use either high performance Generation 2 or Generation 2I image intensifiers to provide selective performance. AQUILA 2I is constructed of high strength aluminum alloy designed to survive rugged military use and environments. The external surfaces are a non-reflective matte black finish. AQUILA 2I is designed for mounting on a wide range of weapons incorporating STANAG or Weaver mounts. It can also be mounted to meet customer specifications. Controls and adjustments are simple to operate. The system is activated by a ON/OFF lever. The red LED reticle brightness is controlled by an adjustment knob. A focus ring is located on the eyepiece for individual eye adjustment. A seperate objective focus knob is located on the forward portion of the sight body for adjustment from 25 meters to infinity. The AQUILA 2I uses two "AA" batteries to insure longer operational performance between changes.
