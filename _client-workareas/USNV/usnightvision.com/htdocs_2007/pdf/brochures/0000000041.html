The Blackout™ IR Spot Light kit includes a million candle power professional grade spotlight with a detachable Infrared filter. The spotlight is cordless and includes a DC cigarette lighter power cord and an AC charger. When used in conjunction with night vision the Blackout™ Infrared Spotlight will illuminate any target with ease. Regardless of the Generation, this kit is specifically designed to enhance performance of any night vision device, and be quickly converted to spot light for normal nighttime use without night vision.
