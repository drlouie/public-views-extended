The USNV-14B utilizes two US Made ITT Generation 3 (64lp) image intensifier tubes to provide crisp, clear images under the darkest conditions. Utilizing the most advanced Mil. Spec. housing, this system is built to handle the toughest of environments.

The dual tube binocular design offers greater performance than a single tube design; improved depth perception is an important benefit when it comes to operating vehicles or working on equipment at night.

The USNV-14B can be hand held, or attached to a standard PVS-7 head-mount or helmet mount. Because the USNV-14B is built from two independent monoculars it can be easily separated to create two separate night vision monoculars, or can be attached to a weapon to provide maximum flexibility.

The USNV-14B is smaller and lighter then the any other dual tube system available. The USNV-14B is fully submersible to a depth of 66 feet (20 Meters). The USNV-14B will work with either a single AA or 3.5 volt lithium battery offering more flexibility and longer battery life. The integrated IR illuminator enhances the ability of the user to read maps and operate in confined spaces.
