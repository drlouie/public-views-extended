The USNV-141 weapon sight is built around a high performance Generation 1, enhanced image intensifier. Designed to handle the recoil from a 308 caliber weapon the USNV-141 is built to last. It comes standard with an adjustable white on green reticle, and a variable brightness IR illuminator. Simple to use and highly reliable, the 141 comes standard with a super-strength weaver style mounting base for easy installation to most any weapon. The USNV-141 ships with a soft carry case, a manual and batteries.
