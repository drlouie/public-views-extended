The USNV-303 is a single tube, dual eye goggle that is similar to the Military PVS-7 system. Utilizing US made Generation 2 plus technology, its compact and ergonomic design includes a built in two stage Infrared illuminator with a momentary on off switch. An Infrared indicator and low battery warning is displayed within the user's field of view.

The fully adjustable, padded headgear allows the unit to be flipped-up or quickly detached from the head-mount. An optional 3.2x multicoated lens will convert USNV-303 into a high performance binocular. The USNV-303 is rugged and easy to use, making it a great hands free goggle system for any nighttime application. Ships with unit: Soft carry case, manual and batteries.
