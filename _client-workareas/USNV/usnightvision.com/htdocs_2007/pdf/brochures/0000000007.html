 Hard Case Commercial, Univeral Rail, Weaver, USNV-IR Spot Light Kit
The USNV-241M "Cobra" is built around a high performance US Generation 2 image intensifier. It offers Automatic Brightness Control and Bright Source Protection to safeguard against sudden light overloads, and multi-coated optics that are designed specifically for night vision applications. Other Cobra features include a removable high power, multi-stage infrared light source for sighting in extremely low light conditions, as well as an internally adjustable illuminated reticle for precise target acquisition. Ruggedly built and simple to use, the Cobra utilizes a super strength weaver-style mounting base for easy installation to any modern weapon. Coupled with the camera or camcorder adapter, the Cobra can be also used for night photography. It's the perfect choice for the night vision enthusiast with serious shooting in mind. The USNV-241M ships with a soft carry case, batteries and manual.
