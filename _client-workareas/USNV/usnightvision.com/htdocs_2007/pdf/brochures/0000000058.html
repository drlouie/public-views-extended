The AN/PVS-14D is a light weight, multi-purpose, monocular night viewer. The objective and eyepiece assemblies allow attachment of similar accessories and the head/helmet mount adapter is exactly the same. Also available is a weapon mount adapter to allow the system to be mounted on the M16/M4 Picatinny style rail. A long list of optional accessories allows configuration to meet all customer requirements. The U.S. Army has adopted the AN/PVS-14 as its standard monocular device. It is a self-contained system operating on 2AA batteries and features manual gain control to meet various brightness conditions. A red low-battery indicator appears in the eyepiece when battery replacement is needed. The system also features a projected IR LED for short range illumination for such activities as map reading.

The AN/PVS-14 is supplied with the Military headstrap for hands-free use and has many optional accessories. Each system is built to current U.S. Military specifications and ships with certification of performance specifications from the manufacturer. This package includes the PVS-14D, Military headstrap, sacrificial window, demist shield, batteries, manual and carry case.
