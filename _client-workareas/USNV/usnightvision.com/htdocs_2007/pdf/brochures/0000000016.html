The RAPTOR® is a state-of-the-art, GENERATION 3 night vision weapon sight, utilizing the latest Ultra military image tube providing 21st Century technology. Rugged yet lightweight construction makes the RAPTOR® very reliable and is the most accurate imaging device designed to withstand the rigors of combat use. Originally designed for military and professional demands for extreme long range accuracy, the RAPTOR® can easily be mounted on almost any weapon system including military .50 cal sniper rifles. Optional mount adapters are available for specific mounting needs. Sighting is accomplished with a variable illuminated chevron ballistic reticle. The red on green reticle is fully adjustable for boresighting and has a separate control for optimal reticle brightness and contrast for all lighting conditions. The reticle offers 0.2 Mil elevation and windage adjustments. A daylight cover protects the objective lens, as well as the image tube, against accidental exposure to bright light. This daylight cover can be utilized for training and boresighting during low-light conditions such as dawn, dusk or overcast days. A pressure operated rubber eyeguard provides operator protection from recoil and prevents light scatter from the image tube. All RAPTOR® units ship with an Ultra high performance US military image tube with certification paperwork. Included with each RAPTOR® is a mil-spec throw lever weaver style mount that will attach easily to any weapon as well as hold zero on and off the weapon. Typical operating range is 2425 yards in moonlight / 1695 yards in starlight / 840 yards in cloud cover. Ships with unit, carry case, manual and batteries.
