The USNV-143 is specifically designed for use on today's .22 Rimfires, Adult Airguns, Paintball and Airsoft Guns, the USNV-143 Generation 1 weapon sight offers both quality and performance at an entry-level price point. Available in either Dovetail or Weaver models, the lightweight and compact USNV-143 is ideal for close-range target and pest control applications. Finally, a high-quality night vision weapon sight is available for both Rimfires and Airguns. The USNV-143 ships with a soft carry case, batteries and manual.
