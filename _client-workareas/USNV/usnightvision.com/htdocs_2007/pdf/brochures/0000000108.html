The PVS-14 is the world's most popular Generation 3 monocular. Used currently by the U.S. Armed Forces elite SWAT and Special Ops Units, this monocular includes an IR illuminator, fully adjustable headgear and multi-coated optics. Automatic Brightness Control and Bright Source Protection are standard. Manual gain control allows the user to increase or decrease image tube brightness for best possible image contrast.

The PVS-14 can be used hands-free, weapons mounted, or attached to a camera or camcorder for nighttime photography. Optional accessories include a military helmet mount, illuminated compass, high magnification lenses and weapon's mount. The PVS-14 made by US Night Vision comes standard with a 64LP resolution image tube.
