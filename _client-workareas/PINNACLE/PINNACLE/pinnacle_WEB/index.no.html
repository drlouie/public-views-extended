Pinnacle Artists is a company dedicated to finding the most talented and marketable artists in the country. We then represent and promote the artists we find to fine art galleries across the United States. We understand that art galleries are challenged to find skilled artists that are not over sold, yet still have broad appeal. It is our mission to provide maximum exposure to the artists we represent working through our network of galleries. We have carefully selected our artists based on their talent, existing market appeal, and their dedicated work ethics. We have attempted to find the best artist in different styles in order to offer our clients a broad base of artists to fit their specific market.

Gallery owners are invited to review the portfolios of the artists we represent. We will be happy to discuss exclusive territories and our special promotional packages.
